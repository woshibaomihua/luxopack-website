Frequently Asked Questions

Are the inks and materials truly food-safe?

Yes. We use food-safe soy-based inks and grease-resistant PE or food-grade kraft liner. All materials comply with FDA and EU food contact regulations. Certificates available on request.

Can I get multiple sizes in a single order?

Yes. We can produce multiple sizes in one production run. Each size requires a separate die and carries its own setup fee — we'll quote each SKU individually.

How quickly can I assemble these boxes in a busy kitchen?

The self-locking design assembles in 3–5 seconds per box. No glue, no tape. Staff can preassemble a batch at the start of service or assemble on demand during a rush.

What is the lead time and minimum order?

MOQ is 500 pcs. Lead time is 10–12 business days after final artwork approval. Rush production available for larger orders — ask us for details.
