Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main difference from the standard gable box?

The premium handle version uses 400gsm board (instead of 350gsm) and features a reinforced paper handle designed for heavier items.

Can the handle be a different color?

Yes, handles can be customized to match your branding or even a contrasting color for a unique aesthetic appeal.

Is Spot UV included in the base price?

Spot UV is a finishing option. While it adds slightly to the unit cost, it provides a significant boost to the box's premium look.

How do you ship these to prevent damage?

We ship all gable boxes flat in moisture-proof outer cartons with edge protectors to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.
