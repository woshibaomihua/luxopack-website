Frequently Asked Questions

Are these boxes fully recyclable?

Yes, our corrugated presentation boxes are 100% recyclable and made from post-consumer recycled content.

What is the "textile finish"?

It is a unique texture applied to the kraft surface that mimics the feel of linen or fabric, giving a luxury touch to eco-packaging.

Can I print my logo in full color?

While we recommend eco-friendly soy inks (often 1-2 colors), we can achieve full-color CMYK printing on recycled board.

What is the lead time for eco-boxes?

Production typically takes 12 to 14 days, slightly longer for specialized textures and finishes.
