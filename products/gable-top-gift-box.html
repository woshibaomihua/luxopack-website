Frequently Asked Questions

Does the tear strip work reliably at scale?

Yes. The perforated tear strip is manufactured into the corrugated during production, not added as a separate tape. It is tested for consistent tear resistance across every unit.

Can I print inside the box as well?

Yes. We offer 1–2 color interior printing on the inside base and flaps. Popular for brand messages, thank-you notes or usage instructions.

Is the kraft board truly FSC-certified?

Yes. We hold FSC chain-of-custody certification. We can provide FSC certificates for your compliance documentation upon request.

What sizes are available?

Any size. Send us your product dimensions and we'll engineer the exact box size for a snug, professional fit. Structural dielines provided free of charge.
