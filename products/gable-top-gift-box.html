Frequently Asked Questions

Are these boxes shipped pre-assembled?

No, they are shipped flat to maximize shipping efficiency and reduce storage space. They are very easy to assemble without glue.

What is the weight limit for the 350gsm cardboard?

Depending on the box size, 350gsm board can comfortably hold lightweight items up to 500g-1kg. For heavier items, we recommend our "With Handle" reinforced version.

Can I have a window on the gable box?

Yes, we can add a custom PET or PVC window to showcase the products inside while keeping them secure.

Is the handle strong enough?

The handle is integrated into the structural design of the box, offering excellent durability for its intended use cases.
