Frequently Asked Questions

How durable is the sliding mechanism?

Our sliding drawer boxes are precision-engineered to ensure a smooth, consistent slide throughout the product's life cycle.

What insert options do you offer?

We offer a full range of inserts, including custom-cut EVA foam, high-density sponge, velvet-covered card, and biodegradable molded pulp.

What is the minimum order for custom sizes?

We can manufacture any custom size starting from an MOQ of 100 units.

Can I have different finishes on the sleeve and the drawer?

Absolutely. You can mix and match finishes—for example, a matte sleeve with a gold-foiled drawer for a striking contrast.
