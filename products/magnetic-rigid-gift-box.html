Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our standard MOQ for custom magnetic gift boxes is 100 units. This allows small brands to access premium packaging.

How does the pricing vary with quantity?

Unit costs decrease significantly at higher volumes due to economies of scale in setup and production. 5000+ units offer the best value.

What is the standard production lead time?

Production typically takes 12 to 15 business days after artwork approval. Shipping time depends on your location and method (Air vs. Sea).

Simply contact us with your dimensions and design requirements. We provide a free 3D mockup to help you visualize the final product.
