Finishing Options

Same Box. Completely Different Feel.

Every finishing technique changes how your packaging feels and the story it tells. Choose the one that matches your brand's personality.

✨
Gold / Silver Foil Stamp
Best for: Luxury, Jewelry, High-end Cosmetics
Metallic hot-stamping applied under heat and pressure. Creates a mirror-finish logo that catches light from every angle. Available in gold, silver, rose gold, holographic.
🖐
Soft Touch Lamination
Best for: Skincare, Fashion, Premium D2C
Velvety matte surface that feels like suede. No glare, deep color saturation. Combine with foil or spot UV for a two-texture contrast effect that buyers can't stop touching.
💠
Spot UV Coating
Best for: Corporate Gifts, Tech Accessories
High-gloss coating applied selectively on top of matte lamination. Makes logos and patterns appear to float above the surface. Tactile and visually striking.
🏔
Embossing / Debossing
Best for: Stationery, Candles, Lifestyle Brands
Raises (emboss) or recesses (deboss) your logo into the board using a custom die. Creates a 3D tactile impression with no ink — pure structural branding.
🌈
Full CMYK Offset Print
Best for: E-Commerce, Subscription Boxes, Food
Photo-quality full-color printing on all panels including interior. Pantone color matching available. The most versatile and cost-effective option for brand-heavy designs.
🎗
Interior Lining & Inserts
Best for: Jewelry, Watches, Electronics, Perfume
Velvet, satin, EVA foam, kraft paper or custom-cut rigid inserts. Holds your product securely and adds a luxury reveal moment. Available in any Pantone color.
