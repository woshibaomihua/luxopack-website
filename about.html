Our Story

Born From a Broken Promise

📦
2009 — The Frustration

A founder who couldn't find a good supplier

Chen Wei spent three years sourcing packaging for his family's premium tea brand in Fujian. Every supplier promised the same thing: quality boxes, on-time delivery, no problems. Every supplier broke that promise at least once. Boxes arrived with crooked printing. Shipments were two weeks late before Chinese New Year. And when he complained, nobody picked up the phone.

He wasn't looking for luxury. He was looking for reliability. He couldn't find it anywhere.

🏭
2010 — The Decision

If no one will build it right, we'll build it ourselves

Chen sold his tea business and used every yuan of the proceeds to lease a 3,000 sqm workshop on the outskirts of Shenzhen. He hired 40 workers — mostly from his home province — and made one rule on day one:

"Every box that leaves this building must be something we would be proud to put our own name on."

He called the company LuxoPack. Not because they were chasing luxury clients — but because lux means light in Latin. He wanted to be the factory that brought clarity and light into an industry known for chaos and broken promises.

🌍
2013–2019 — The Growth

Word travels fast when you keep your promises

LuxoPack's first international client was a small candle brand from Manchester, UK. They found us through a referral — their supplier had dropped the ball and a friend pointed them our way. We delivered 2,000 magnetic boxes on time, zero defects. They reordered six months later with 12,000 units.

That's how most of our clients still find us today — through someone they trust who trusted us first. No cold calls. No trade show gimmicks. Just consistent results that turn clients into advocates. By 2019, LuxoPack was serving 500+ brands across 30 countries and had earned both ISO 9001 and FSC certification — not to win awards, but because our clients' clients were starting to care about where their packaging came from.

✨
Today — The Standard

2,000+ clients. One rule that hasn't changed

Today LuxoPack operates from a 30,000 sqm factory with 500+ staff and ships over 50 million boxes a year to brands in 50+ countries. We've grown — but Chen's original rule is still posted on the wall of every production floor.

"Every box that leaves this building must be something we would be proud to put our own name on."

We still pick up the phone. We still meet our deadlines. And we still believe that the best packaging isn't just beautiful — it's dependable.

"

I started LuxoPack because I was tired of being lied to by packaging factories. I wanted to build the company I wished existed when I was on the other side of the table. Fifteen years later, that's still the only brief I care about.

👤
Chen Wei
Founder & CEO, LuxoPack
