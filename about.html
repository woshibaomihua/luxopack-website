Our Story

Born from a Belief That Packaging Matters

LuxoPack was founded in 2010 by a team of packaging engineers with a simple belief: the box a product comes in is not an afterthought — it is the first chapter of the brand story.

Starting from a 3,000sqm workshop with 40 staff, we have grown into one of South China's leading premium packaging manufacturers, with a 30,000sqm facility, 500+ employees, and clients in over 50 countries.

We remain factory-direct — no agents, no middlemen. When you work with LuxoPack, you work directly with the people designing, printing, and assembling your packaging every day.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

15 years of growth, investment and innovation — always with quality at the center.

2010
LuxoPack Founded
Started with 40 staff and a 3,000sqm workshop in Shenzhen, producing basic gift boxes for local wholesalers.
2013
First Export Orders
Secured first international clients from the US and UK. Invested in CMYK offset printing equipment to meet global quality standards.
2016
ISO 9001 Certification
Achieved ISO 9001 quality management certification. Expanded to 12,000sqm. Launched rigid box and magnetic closure product lines.
2019
FSC & SGS Certified
Obtained FSC certification for sustainable sourcing. Added 8 new production lines. Client base grew to 500+ brands in 30 countries.
2022
New 30,000sqm Facility
Moved into brand new purpose-built factory. Installed automated die-cutting and gluing machinery. Staff reached 400+.
2026
2,000+ Global Clients
Today we serve 2,000+ brands across 50+ countries, producing over 50 million boxes per year. Still family-owned. Still factory-direct.
